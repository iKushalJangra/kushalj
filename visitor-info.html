Visitor Guidelines

To ensure the safety and comfort of all patients and visitors, please follow these guidelines

Hospital Policies

Visitor Check-In

All visitors must check in at the information desk and wear a visitor badge while in the hospital.

Patient Safety

Visitors with colds, sore throats or any contagious diseases should not visit patients.

Smoking Policy

HealthPlus is a smoke-free campus. Smoking is not permitted anywhere on hospital property.

Cell Phone Use

Cell phones may be used in most areas except where posted (ICU, NICU, etc.). Please be considerate of others.

Photography

Photography is prohibited in patient care areas without prior authorization.

Gifts for Patients

Please check with nursing staff before bringing food, flowers or gifts to patient rooms.
